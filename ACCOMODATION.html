Grand Sierra Pines

Grand Sierra Pines Hotel is Baguio City’s #1 hotel. Located on the quieter side of the city, the 60 room hotel offers relaxing accommodations, unique facilities, and dining choices for guests to have a redefined Baguio experience!

Garding Wing

At the heart of Camp John Hay’s historic estate, the Garden Wing rises as a four-storey mountain chalet in Baguio that evokes the grandeur of old-world elegance. Here, classic alpine architecture is reimagined with Filipino artistry, pinewood and Benguet stone finishes, shingled gable roofs, and expansive balconies that open onto sweeping gardens and panoramic views of the Cordillera mountain range.
