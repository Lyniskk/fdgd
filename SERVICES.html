Tourism

A major economic driver, providing a large number of jobs and services related to hospitality and recreation. Tourism services in Baguio include government resources like the Baguio City Tourism and Special Events Office (CTSEO) for visitor assistance, and private sector options such as registered tour guides from the Baguio Tourism Council, travel agencies offering packages, hotel bookings, and airline ticketing.

Professional & Business Services

This is the fastest-growing industry in Baguio.Professional and business services in Baguio include accounting, business process outsourcing (BPO), and consulting, with the central business district on Session Road being a major hub for various enterprises. For businesses, services can range from IT solutions to tax and permit assistance provided by local government and private firms.

Wholesale and Retail Trade

The second-largest sector in the city's economy. Baguio has a vibrant wholesale and retail trade sector, with wholesale, retail trade, and repair of motor vehicles and motorcycles being a significant part of its economy. Common retail activities include selling local products like strawberries and other produce, as well as the famous "ukay-ukay" (thrift store) items found at the night market. Businesses can register for permits through the city's online system or the Business Permit and Licensing Division on Facebook.
