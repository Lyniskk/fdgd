Through Bus

Victory Liner: Offers a 24-hour service with frequent trips from terminals like Pasay, Cubao, and Caloocan. They have both regular and first-class options, which is available from their Pasay and Cubao terminals. Genesis Transport (JoyBus): This company provides a comfortable travel experience, with its JoyBus service being a popular option for non-stop trips to Baguio. You can book tickets online through sites like iWantSeats or the JoyBus website.

Through Private Vehicle

Route: The most common and convenient route is through the Tarlac-Pangasinan-La Union Expressway (TPLEX). Estimated time: Travel time is usually between 4 and 6 hours, depending on traffic conditions. Alternative route: For a different lane experience, you can take Marcos Highway, known for its wides and scenic views.
