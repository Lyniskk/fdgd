Burnham Park

This historic urban park is a central hub for recreation. It features a lake where you can rent boats, gardens, and biking paths, designed by its namesake, American architect Daniel Burnham.

Mines View Park

Known for its stunning panoramic views of the mountains and valleys, this park is also a popular spot to take photos and experience the city's scenic beauty

The Mansion

This is the official summer palace of the President of the Philippines, making it a significant historical and architectural landmark to visit.
