Savory Dishes

Baguio Bagnet: A must-try, this crispy, deep-fried pork belly is a local specialty. Buttered Chicken: A popular and budget-friendly dish, especially at Good Taste Restaurant, known for its buttery marinade and garlic toppings. Cordilleran Cuisine: Sample traditional dishes like pinikpikan (smoked chicken) and smoked pork at places like Farmer's Daughter. Agara Ramen: For those craving a warm, comforting bowl of ramen, Agara Ramen is a popular choice.
